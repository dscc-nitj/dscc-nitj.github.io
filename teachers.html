About NITJ

Dr BR Ambedkar National Institute of Technology was established in the year 1987 as Regional Engineering College and was given the status of National Institute of Technology (Deemed University) by the Government of India on October 17, 2002, under the aegis of the Ministry of Human Resource Development, New Delhi. Now the Ministry of Education, Government of India has declared the Institute as “Institute of National Importance” under the act of Parliament-2007. The Institute offers BTech, MTech, MSc, MBA and PhD programmes in the several disciplines of Engineering, Technology and Sciences. The Institute is committed to developing technical human resource of excellence suitable for global requirements. The Institute ranked 52nd amongst the top 200 Indian institutes and 12th among the 31 NITs in NIRF ranking 2020. For more info: http://www.nitj.ac.in

About CSE Department

Established in 1990, the Department of Computer Science & Engineering is well recognized for excellence in facilities and teaching. Department has research orientation in various fields of Computer Science. Department has presently 20 faculty members with a PhD degree from reputed Institutions. Faculty members and research scholars of the Department are actively involved in research activities and have various publications in journals of international repute. The key research areas of the Department are in the field of Data Science, Data Mining, Cloud computing, Wireless Networks, IoT, Software engineering, Security, etc. Project-based learning is the prime focus of the department. The CSED always yields towards the betterment of students by deploying excellent teaching-learning process, alumni interaction, and industry collaborations.

A message from the director

With the exponential growth of data from various sources including social sites, the era of Big Data emerged, in a big way which has necessitated the need for data analytics decision thereof. Data Science is about finding patterns in data and make future predictions. Applications of data science are growing day by day in many fields such as business, science, industry, research, governance, etc. You have a large amount of data and you’re trying to extract something smart and useful from it. Just consider the current scenario of the pandemic, decisions involving less risk in less time are required to handle the situation in time. The use of data analytics is one of the best options to make correct decisions. Similarly, many problems can be seen in our day-to-day life. With the exponential increase in the data, the right tools and techniques to get the required results have been highly desired. The field of data science is not only about programming, it is a full bundle of your analytical, logical as well as mathematical skills used to decode the particular problem. It helps to extract meaningful insights to identify issues and the space that needs to be worked upon to make it better. This summer school will provide a push start to the students in the field of Data Science. Best wishes to the organization team and the participants. I hope you will have a great learning experience.

About The School

An intensive One-month summer school is being organized for the 1st and 2nd year students of engineering and technological institutions. It is also open to persons from industry and doctoral/MTech/BTech students. The basic motive of this code camp is to provide the basic knowledge of the primary tools of the data science domain and provide hands on experience to the students at very initial. This summer school will provide students a platform for data science by providing project-based curriculum.
